Healthy Health

How bad is your health ? worry no more because HEALTH TIPS is at your door step. All you need is just a guidline to get your health healthy again.

Be active

Physical activity is any bodily movement produced by skeletal muscles that requires energy expenditure. This includes exercise and activities undertaken while working, playing,and engaging in recreational pursuits.

Eat a healthy diet

Eat a combination of different foods, including fruit, vegetables, legumes, nuts and whole grains. Adults should eat at least five portions (400g) of fruit and vegetables per day.

Don't smoke

Smoking tobacco causes NCDs such as lung disease, heart disease and stroke. Tobacco kills not only the direct smokers but even non-smokers through second-hand exposure.

Consume less salt and sugar

High intake of sodium increases the risk of heart disease and stroke.Reduce your salt intake to 5g per day, equivalent to about one teaspoon.

Should I cut carbohydrates?

Instead of completely cutting out carbs, I advise my clients to focus on the quality and quantity of carbs they're eating. Carbs aren't all white bread, pasta and sweets—the carb food group also includes whole grains, fruit, vegetables and legumes. NB: "minimally processed, high-quality carbs provide us with energy and are loaded with fiber, vitamins, minerals and antioxidants," says Virga.

Do I need to only eat organic foods to be healthy?

Eating foods labeled as organic reduces your exposure to synthetic pesticides—but nutritionally, candy is candy whether it's labeled organic or not."Vegetables and fruits should be consumed on a regular basis to promote optimal health," says Brooklyn-based registered dietitian-nutritionist Maya Feller, RD. I generally tell my patients to make a balanced choice that's sustainable rather than if the food's organic or conventional.
